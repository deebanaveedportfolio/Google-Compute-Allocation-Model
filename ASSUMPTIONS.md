# Model Assumptions

This simulation is an independent analytical exercise based solely on publicly available information.

The model intentionally simplifies several aspects of large-scale AI infrastructure.

Key assumptions include:

- Compute demand is represented using normalized units rather than actual TPU-hours.
- Capacity constraints are hypothetical.
- Shadow prices illustrate opportunity cost rather than internal transfer pricing.
- The objective is to explore strategic resource-allocation trade-offs rather than estimate Google's internal operations.
